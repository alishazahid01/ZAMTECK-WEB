Emporio Armani Cafe

Sowwah Square, Abu Dhabi

Project Overview

The Emporio Armani Cafe project involved the complete MEP design and installation for this high-end dining establishment. Our team was responsible for creating a sophisticated environment that matched the Armani brand's luxury aesthetic while ensuring optimal functionality.

The project included state-of-the-art kitchen facilities, custom lighting design, and advanced climate control systems to ensure guest comfort in all seasons.

Key Features

  • Custom-designed HVAC system with zoned temperature control
  • Energy-efficient LED lighting with programmable scenes
  • Commercial kitchen ventilation and fire suppression systems
  • Building management system integration for centralized control
  • Water-efficient plumbing fixtures and systems
  • Backup power systems for critical operations

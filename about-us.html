About Us

Building A Legacy Of
Competence, Dedication, And Trust

Established in 2007, ZEMTECK Electromechanical Works is ISO certified and one of the leading electromechanical contractors in the GCC. Specialized in the execution of commercial, industrial, and residential projects, the company has a key focus on fast track projects. Professional excellence, reliability, and timely completion of jobs are characteristics that make ZEMTECK Electromechanical Works LLC a leading electromechanical contractor in the region.

Our team of highly qualified and experienced engineers and technicians are committed to delivering the highest quality of work, ensuring that all projects are completed to the highest standards and within the agreed timeframe.
